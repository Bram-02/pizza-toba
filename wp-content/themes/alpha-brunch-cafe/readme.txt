== Description ==

The Alpha Brunch Cafe WordPress Theme is a stylish and versatile solution designed specifically for cafes, restaurants, coffee shops, juice bars, and other food service establishments. Whether you're offering breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, or desserts, this theme is ideal for any dining venue. Perfect for cozy coffee shops, vibrant juice bars, sophisticated tea houses, or trendy brunch spots, Alpha Brunch Cafe provides an elegant and functional platform to present your menu and services. Featuring a clean, modern design, the theme enhances the visual appeal of your offerings, allowing you to showcase everything from specialty coffee and organic blends to craft teas and artisanal pastries. Easily highlight popular items such as espresso, cappuccino, lattes, mochas, and a variety of teas, as well as fresh juices, smoothies, and cocktails. With its responsive and mobile-friendly design, Alpha Brunch Cafe ensures your website looks stunning across all devices, making it simple for customers to explore your menu, place orders, or make reservations. The themeâ€™s vibrant images, customizable sections, and intuitive layout allow you to emphasize your unique dining experience, whether you're featuring gourmet sandwiches, salads, tapas, charcuterie boards, or decadent desserts. Designed with food service establishments in mind, this theme provides all the tools needed to enhance your online presence and engage customers effectively, ensuring your cafe or restaurant stands out in a competitive market.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
